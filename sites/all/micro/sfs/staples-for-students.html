This summer, Staples and DoSomething.org will host our fourth annual Staples for Students School Supply Drive – a campaign mobilizing young people to collect school supplies for the 13 million kids who return to school without basic supplies.

  • From July 3 through Sept 17, you can collect school supplies and drop them off at your local Staples store.
  • Team up with the cast, spread the word, and enter for a chance to win amazing prizes.
